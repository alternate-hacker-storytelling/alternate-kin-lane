About Alternate Kin Lane

While immersed in a world of technology, reading and writing about real-world technologies, I needed an outlet to put my fictional writing.

I find it easier to get back to the every day grind of tech blogging, if I have a place to produce ficitional work. So I created an alternate reality where I can blog about the fake shit I see every day.

Nothing you read at alternate.kinlane.com is real, if you are looking for my non-fiction work visit kinlane.com

If you have any questions you can email me at kinlane@gmail.com or tweet @kinlane.

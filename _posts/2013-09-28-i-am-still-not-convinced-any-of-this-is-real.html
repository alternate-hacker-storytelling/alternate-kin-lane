The more I progress through life, I'm increasingly convinced that none of this is real. As soon as I left the small town I was raised in, I began seeing signs that this was all just a video game extension of the small town arcade I used to blow all my quarters in at the other end of town.

I got a taste of virtual reality, programming computers in the 80's, even before the Internet was widely available--giving me some awareness that this was truly a reality show version of the game of civilization.

While I hit many speed bumps through out my early years I quickly learned i could manifest what I needed, albeit sometimes this was a good ass kicking and night spent sleeping in a ditch. The powers of manifestation isn't something they teach you in school, it is something you have to be introduced to incrementally through loving instruction and simultaneous pain, suffering and torture.

After learning I could apply Jedi mind tricks on individuals ranging from criminals to law enforcement I quickly realized I need to exercise my skills in a much more safer environment before I pushed things too far, and possibly exploded in a ball of flames.

After several years of working out in a friendly, small town work environment I thought my financial reality was a good place to test out my skills. Starting at about 30K annual income I wanted to see what I could expand this to in a mater of 5 years, ultimately taking things to 150K before I knew that I had what it would take to go the distance, but money wasn't what interested me.

Next I wanted to see if I could expand on the scope of the the world in which I was exposed to on a geographical. I wanted to be able to command things at not just a national level, but take it global and see what I could make happen. This obviously would take flexing the financial side of things, but also would take reaching a new scope of the characters I interacted with, requiring time, patience and good ol fashion relationship building.

This is where I stand. I'm able to rise to a national level, getting the attention of national leaders in government and corporations. I've successfully attracted global forces in technology and across multiple other business sectors., and now its just a matter of understanding what I can do next.

Pushing the boundaries of reality have shown me that none of this is real. It is al just a matter of understanding the appropriate game mechanics and physics at play in this reality and understand which level I'm currently on.

Let's see what I can manifest next, and where we can take all of this.

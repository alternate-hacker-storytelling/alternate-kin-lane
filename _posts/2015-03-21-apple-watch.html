We are watching you. We are watching everything you do. We are tracking all of your data, from your location, to the apps you use, and the pictures and videos you take. We watch everything you do—we just wanted to let you know.

You are under the watchful gaze of Apple Watch, not in some disconnected surveillance sense, but like a mother watching over her children—Apple is watching you. We want to understand, and know you better so we can better serve yo. This is the Apple watch.
